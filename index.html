About Me

I'm Émilie, a computer engineering student with a strong interest in the software side of tech, especially front-end development and UX design. I've always admired clean aesthetics and thoughtful design, which naturally drew me to creating interfaces that are both functional and visually appealing.

Alongside design, I’m also curious about how things work behind the scenes, like building software systems to exploring cybersecurity and how to protect them. Whether I’m coding interfaces or digging into the backend, I enjoy learning how all the parts of a system connect and work together. I like working on projects that combine creativity and logic, and I’m always looking for new ways to grow as a developer and designer.
